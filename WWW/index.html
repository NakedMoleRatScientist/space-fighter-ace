Space Fighter Ace

Space Fighter Ace is a space shooting game. Currently, it is a simple game. You engage the enemies and kill them before they kill you. It is programmed entirely in Ruby and use the Rubygame library.

Version

As of December 5, 2006, the latest version is 0.0.1. You can download the game on Space Fighter Ace's Rubyforge.org project page.
